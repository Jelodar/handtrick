ADVANCED

Combined routing for regions, exclusive sequences, bare paths, wheel, keyboard roles, rolling tap, modifier pan, pinch, and guarded rotate.

TAPTap center, edges, and corners. Double-tap style chains stay deterministic when a longer sequence is armed.
SEQTap, tap, then swipe right. The sequence wins; direct tap and doubletap do not also fire.
KEYSAlt+tap/swipe acts as two fingers. Ctrl+tap acts as three. Meta-click a short line for keyboard roll.
ROLLRoll two or three fingers left/right with staggered downs. Wider spacing and delayed waves are accepted.
MODHold one pointer, tap or drag another. Shift+drag mirrors modifier pan on desktop.
PATHDraw left then down while held. Draw down then right for the second command.
VIEWPinch or wheel to scale. Twist deliberately to inspect rebased rotation and raw rotation.
